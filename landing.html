Voice-to-Text Capture

Invite crew members by email. Assign each one to a single department. They only see their work. Admins see everything.

How It Works

From setup to your first report in minutes.

MuniVault is built so non-technical city staff can get started without IT support.

1
Set up your city
Create an admin account, name your city, upload your logo, and pick your departments. Takes about 3 minutes.
2
Invite your crew
Add crew members by name and email. Each gets a unique invite code and is assigned to one department.
3
Crew captures activity
Crews tap "New Capture", talk into their phone, snap photos. Everything's tagged with location and time.
4
Export & report
Admin downloads everything to CSV, JSON, or photo bundles. Hand it to council, auditors, or the public.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to install anything? +
No. MuniVault is a Progressive Web App that runs in any modern browser. On a phone, you can save it to your home screen and it'll work like a native app — but there's nothing to download from an app store, and no IT setup required.
How does the free trial work? +
You get full access to the Starter tier for 14 days — no credit card required. A countdown banner shows how many days you have left. At the end, you choose a plan (or walk away with no obligation).
Who can sign up to use my city's MuniVault? +
Only people you invite. There's no public sign-up form. The admin generates a unique invite code for each crew member and shares it with them. Without a code, nobody can create an account on your city's vault.
What does a "crew member" see? +
Crew members are scoped to one department. They can only see and capture entries for their assigned department — they can't view other departments' activity or access any admin features. Admins see everything across the city.
Can I add more seats than my plan includes? +
Yes. Extra crew member seats are $10/month each on any plan. You can add or remove them anytime through the billing portal.
Where is my city's data stored? +
Securely in our cloud database (Supabase, hosted in the US), with row-level security so no other city can see your data. You can export everything to CSV, JSON, or photo bundles at any time — your data is always yours.
What if I cancel? +
You can cancel anytime through the billing portal. Before you do, export your data (CSV, JSON, photos) so you have a permanent local copy. Your account remains accessible through the end of the billing period.
